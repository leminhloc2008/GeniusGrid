Autism Awareness

Understanding Autism Spectrum Disorder

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) affects millions of individuals worldwide. Here are some key statistics and information to help increase awareness and understanding.

Prevalence

According to the CDC, approximately 1 in 54 children in the U.S. is diagnosed with ASD.

Gender Distribution

ASD is about 4 times more common among boys than among girls.

Early Intervention

2 years old

ASD can be reliably diagnosed as early as age 2, allowing for early intervention which can significantly improve outcomes.

Common Signs of ASD

  • Difficulty with social interaction and communication
  • Restricted or repetitive behaviors and interests
  • Challenges with verbal and non-verbal communication
  • Difficulty understanding other people's feelings or perspectives
  • Sensory sensitivities (e.g., to sounds, lights, textures)
